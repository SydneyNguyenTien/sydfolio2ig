Another day another crochet

My first duck project

It was on June 12th 2022 that my first duck came to life with his fluffy fur, squeaky beak, and bouncy eyes that were not glued (left). He was not my best creation, but he was my fist. On the next few days, I began to master my duckling techniques and create more of the duckies. One duck was made to honor my idol in the CS field with the initial W. on his body. Other ducks were made for my friends. Soon, I have a flock of ducks.

Over my journey of producing ducks, I altered the original crochet pattern to arrive at my final duck design (right). He has his eyes closed and lack visible wings, and he ressembles a rubber duck that reminds me of the self-teaching technique in CS. I began to talk to the ducks everytime I got stuck on a problem.
